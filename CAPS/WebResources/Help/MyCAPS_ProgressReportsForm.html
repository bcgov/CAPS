Progress Reports

s reports are automatically created on the 1st of each month for Projects that have completed their CPFA/APFA Approval.

School District users are to complete and submit the record within one week.

Ministry Planning Officers can then refer to the updated information provided by the district to update the related Project record as needed.

Milestone Dates are auto-set and locked if already completed. Otherwise provide the expected date.

The Monthly Project Progress report embedded at the bottom of the form provides an easy place to see past progress report details to aid in filling out this report.
