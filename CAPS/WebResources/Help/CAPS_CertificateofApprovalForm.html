How is the Max COA Amount ($) field calculated?

  • For Major the Max COA Amount ($) is calculated by taking the sum of Total Approved amount and Federal amount found on the Project, less the Total Actual Draws on Previous COAs ($).

  • For Minor the Max COA Amount ($) is calculated by taking the Initial Approved amount found on the Project, less the Total Actual Draws on Previous COAs ($).

  • For AFG COA's only, the Max COA Amount ($) is equal to the Capital Portion found on the Project, less Total Actual Draws on Previous COAs ($).
